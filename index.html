    Small simple fool-your-friends (FAKE) hacking app.

    This app has a text box for an IP and two radio buttons (those all do nothing.) Then you click a button which actually does something. It opens a window that loads a page at hackertyper.net. (the window will look white for a while but it is just loading.) When the window turns black and there is a green cursor you are ready. Then you just press random keys and it types stuff. Press the ALT key three times after a min or so. Then just point at the screen and say "aha!" Click the exit button. Go to a website or logout of your computer or in some other way get to a login box for which you can login to and login. Your freind(s) will think you just hacked the login and you saw the password when you pointed at the screen.
